BOOM! Power Rangers

Created Sunday 11 July 2021

Back in 2016, comic book publisher Boom Studios launched their line of Power Rangers comics. They mixed classic concepts from the first season with plenty of new ideas.

How are the comics different from one another?
Similar to the TV show, where there are distinct versions of the rangers, the comics have established a multiverse where different teams can exist. The main title, Mighty Morphin Power Rangers (MMPR), focuses on a modern interpretation of the original line-up. While the secondary title, Go Go Power Rangers (GGPR), focuses on the original team that’s rooted in the 90s. Apart from a few instances where they crossover with each other, they tell separate stories and can be read independently.

Unlimited Power Era

From November 2020, Boom Studios is relaunching the Power Rangers comics as two concurrent series – Mighty Morphin and Power Rangers. Mighty Morphin will focus on a cast that’s closer to season 2 of the show. Power Rangers will focus on the original line-up, now known as the Omega Rangers.
As they’re so new, it’s not known at the moment how much these two comics will intertwine with each other. I’ll update this section once we get a better picture of what’s going on.
